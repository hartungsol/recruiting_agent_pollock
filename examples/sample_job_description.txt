OTR CDL-A Truck Driver

Company: Midwest Freight Lines

Location: Midwest Region (Home base: Kansas City, MO)

Job Type: Full-time

Compensation: $0.55-$0.65 per mile + bonuses

About the Position:
We are seeking experienced OTR CDL-A drivers to join our growing fleet. This position offers consistent miles, competitive pay, and excellent home time for regional drivers.

Requirements:
- Valid Class A CDL
- Minimum 2 years of verifiable OTR experience
- Clean MVR (no more than 2 moving violations in the last 3 years)
- No DUI/DWI in the last 5 years
- No at-fault accidents in the last 3 years
- Must pass DOT physical and drug screening
- Ability to lift up to 50 lbs

Disqualifying Factors:
- Any DUI/DWI within the past 5 years
- More than 2 moving violations in the last 3 years
- Failed drug test or refusal to test
- Felony conviction within the last 7 years
- No valid CDL-A license

Preferred Qualifications:
- Tanker endorsement
- Hazmat endorsement
- Experience with electronic logging devices (ELD)
- Doubles/triples certification
- Regional or dedicated route experience

Equipment:
- Late model Freightliner Cascadias
- All trucks equipped with APU, refrigerator, and inverter
- Automatic and manual transmission options available

Benefits:
- Weekly home time (out 5-6 days, home 2 days)
- Health, dental, and vision insurance
- 401(k) with company match
- Paid vacation after 1 year
- Rider and pet policy
- Direct deposit

Schedule:
- Out 5-6 days, home for reset
- No-touch freight (90% drop and hook)
- Consistent lanes and dedicated customers
